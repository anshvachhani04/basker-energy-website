Origin story

Why we started BASKER.

2023 — INSIGHT

The energy world is fragmented; software is fragmented; AI is everywhere except where it's needed most.

Our founder spent 18 months interviewing 140+ utility CEOs, plant managers, regulators and energy traders. The same complaint kept surfacing: "We need fewer vendors and more intelligence."

2024 — FOUNDED

BASKER Energy is incorporated in Mumbai.

The original 8-person engineering team begins building the AI Energy Cloud and the first three solution modules.

2024 — FIRST PILOT

First production pilot at a 280 MW industrial facility.

Reduced energy intensity 24% in 9 months. Validates the Industrial Energy OS thesis.

2025 — BCG PARTNERSHIP

BCG joins as strategic partner and advisor.

Joint go-to-market across heavy industry, utilities and government across India, MENA and ASEAN.

2026 — TODAY

1,400 tenants. 6.4 GW under management. 12 modules shipped.

BASKER stands at the inflection point of the AI-energy convergence — and is hiring across all teams.
